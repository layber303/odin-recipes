The most delicious dish in the entire universe

This meal is considered to be on of the if not THE BEST food in the universe, it has a perfect mixture of cheddar cheese and just the right amount of bacon, to make it blend perfectly and create an explosion in your mouth

Ingredients:

  • Bun
  • Meat
  • Cheddar
  • Bacon

Steps:

  1. Put the meat into the grill
  2. After a couple of minutes put teh cheddar on top of the meat and the buns and bacon on the side
  3. Put the meat and bacon between the buns
  4. Enjoy
